## Revision note

The original version of this file (teal/cyan + green, Figtree/Noto Sans) was generated from the
`ui-ux-pro-max` skill's generic healthcare/medical-clinic color recommendation. That palette is
the skill's default answer for *every* healthcare query (verified by re-querying it directly) ‚Äî
it doesn't reflect anything specific to this clinic. This revision deliberately departs from it
to use the clinic's own name ("Shimmering Meadow") as the design's anchor: a warm, botanical,
sunlit identity instead of a generic clinical-teal one. Tokens below are hand-authored, not
generated by the search tool ‚Äî do not regenerate this file via `--persist` without preserving
this direction.

## Signature Element

A hand-drawn-style grass/wildflower line illustration is the page's one real signature ‚Äî used
deliberately and sparingly (per the frontend-design skill's "spend your boldness in one place"
guidance):

1. **Hero anchor** (`.hero-botanical`) ‚Äî large, full-width silhouette along the bottom edge of
   the hero in `--meadow-800`, with a few blades tipped in `--amber-400` to read as "shimmering."
2. **Footer echo** (`.footer-botanical`) ‚Äî a small single-line row of grass blades at the top of
   the footer, in `--meadow-400` against the dark footer background, tying the page's opening and
   closing moments together.

No other decorative shapes exist on the page ‚Äî the previous generic white circle blobs in the
hero and CTA band were removed rather than recolored.

## Style Guidelines

**Style:** Warm Botanical Trust ‚Äî a deliberate departure from generic "Accessible & Ethical
medical SaaS" (still keeps all of that category's accessibility rigor, see checklist below, just
not its visual clich√©s).

**Best For:** This specific clinic ‚Äî do not reuse this exact palette/illustration for unrelated
healthcare projects; it's anchored to the "Shimmering Meadow" name.

### Page Pattern

- **Conversion strategy:** Trust strip (24/7 support, board-certified, patient count, rating),
  stats band, testimonials with real ratings, lead magnet (free preventive-care checklist) as a
  mid-funnel capture point, low-friction appointment form.
- **CTA label:** Standardized to **"Book an Appointment"** everywhere (nav, hero, about, CTA
  band) ‚Äî previously inconsistent ("Book Appointment" / "Book Your Appointment" in different
  places). Contact form submit button reads **"Request Appointment"** to match its section
  heading ("Request an Appointment").
- **Section order:** Nav ‚Üí Hero ‚Üí Stats ‚Üí About ‚Üí Services ‚Üí Testimonials ‚Üí Lead Magnet ‚Üí CTA
  band ‚Üí Contact ‚Üí Footer.

## Anti-Patterns (Do NOT Use)

- ‚ùå The generic "medical teal + green" palette (this skill's own default recommendation for
  healthcare ‚Äî deliberately not used here)
- ‚ùå "Cream background + serif + terracotta" combo (flagged AI-clich√© ‚Äî avoided by staying
  green-dominant with amber, not terracotta, as the accent)
- ‚ùå Bright neon colors
- ‚ùå AI purple/pink gradients
- ‚ùå Decorative shapes that aren't the grass/wildflower signature (no random circles/blobs)

### Additional Forbidden Patterns

- ‚ùå **Emojis as icons** ‚Äî Use SVG icons (already all inline SVG, currentColor stroke)
- ‚ùå **Missing cursor:pointer** ‚Äî All clickable elements must have cursor:pointer
- ‚ùå **Layout-shifting hovers** ‚Äî Avoid scale transforms that shift layout
- ‚ùå **Low contrast text** ‚Äî Maintain 4.5:1 minimum contrast ratio
- ‚ùå **Instant state changes** ‚Äî Always use transitions (150-300ms)
- ‚ùå **Invisible focus states** ‚Äî Dark ring (`--meadow-900`) on light sections, white ring override
  on dark sections (footer, stats, CTA band, trust strip)
